About Farm Master Lite

Who We Are

Farm Master Lite v1.0 is a comprehensive farming calculator app designed specifically for Nigerian farmers. Built with love for the farming community, we support 20 major Nigerian crops and provide calculations based on local farming conditions and best practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate, offline-accessible tools that help farmers make informed decisions about fertilizer application, water management, and yield estimation. We believe that every farmer deserves access to professional-grade farming tools without the need for expensive software or internet connectivity.

What Makes Us Different

  • Offline-First Design: All calculations work without internet - perfect for rural farming areas
  • Nigerian-Focused: Our crop database and calculations are specifically tailored for Nigerian farming conditions
  • Privacy-Focused: Your data never leaves your device - complete privacy and security
  • Free to Use: No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no data collection
  • Farmer-Friendly: Simple, intuitive interface designed for farmers of all technical levels

Built for Nigerian Agriculture

Farm Master Lite is built specifically for the Nigerian farming community. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in Nigerian agriculture, and our tools reflect local farming practices, crop varieties, and environmental conditions.
