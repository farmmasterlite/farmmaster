Farm Master Lite - Your Smart Farming Companion

Calculate fertilizer needs, manage water resources, and estimate yields for your crops - all offline, all free.

How the App Works

Farm Master Lite is a comprehensive farming calculator app designed specifically for Nigerian farmers. Our mission is to provide accurate, offline-accessible tools that help farmers make informed decisions about fertilizer application, water management, and yield estimation.

The App Features Three Main Tools

Fertilizer Calculator

  • Select your crop from 20+ Nigerian crops
  • Enter your farm area (hectares, acres, or square meters)
  • Select soil fertility level (low, normal, or high)
  • Get NPK fertilizer requirements and bag counts
  • Calculate seed quantities needed

Water Calculator

  • Choose your crop and farm area
  • Select water need level (dry/normal/wet conditions)
  • Pick your irrigation system (flood, furrow, sprinkler, or drip)
  • Get weekly and monthly water requirements in liters and cubic meters

Yield Estimator

  • Select crop and farm area
  • Choose performance level (poor, average, good, or excellent)
  • View estimated harvest yields
  • Compare different performance scenarios
