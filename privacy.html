Privacy Policy - Farm Master Lite

Your Privacy Matters

Farm Master Lite is designed with privacy in mind:

NO DATA COLLECTION

We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your farming data never leaves your device.

OFFLINE FIRST

All calculations happen on your device. No internet connection is required for the app to function.

NO TRACKING

We don't track your usage or farming data. There are no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking pixels.

NO ACCOUNTS

No sign-up required, no personal data stored. You can start using the app immediately without creating an account.

LOCAL STORAGE

All your inputs stay on your device. We don't have access to your calculations, crop selections, or farm data.

Our Commitment

We believe your farming data should remain private and under your control. Farm Master Lite works completely offline to ensure your information never leaves your device.
